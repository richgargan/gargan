PA Citations[edit]

  • Use these abbreviations only after the full title has been included on the page
    • RONR - Robert's Rules of Order Newly Revised
    • TSC - The Standard Code of Parliamentary Procedure
  • Demeter's Manual (1969) is the 'Blue Book' edition.
  • DEM, MAS, RID are abbreviations used in Template:Cite parl only, not in article text
